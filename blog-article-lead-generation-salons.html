The days of relying on "word of mouth" alone are over. To grow a modern salon, you need a system that brings in new clients predictably. Here’s how to automate your lead generation.

1. Turn Instagram into a Booking Engine

Your Instagram isn't just a portfolio; it's your #1 sales channel. Don't make clients DM you for appointments—that adds friction.

  • Link in Bio: Use a direct booking link (like QTick’s digital profile) so clients can book instantly.
  • Book Button: Add the "Book Now" action button to your profile.
  • Story Links: Post availability in stories with a "Book Here" sticker.

2. Dominate Google Maps

"Salon near me" searches have grown 400% in the last 5 years. If you aren't in the top 3 on Google Maps, you don't exist.

Ensure your Google Business Profile is complete, has your specific services listed as products, and includes your booking link. QTick helps you gather more reviews automatically, which boosts your ranking.

3. A Website that Converts

Your website shouldn't just be pretty; it should be a funnel.

  • Clear CTA: The "Book Now" button should be visible without scrolling.
  • Lead Magnet: Offer a "10% Off First Visit" coupon in exchange for their phone number. This lets you retarget them via WiFi marketing or WhatsApp.

4. Automate Referrals

Happy clients are your best marketers. But they often forget to refer friends.

Set up an automated WhatsApp message that goes out 2 hours after a 5-star appointment: "Loving your new look? Share this link with a friend and you BOTH get $10 off your next visit!"
