Paper receipts fade. They get lost. And they are a pain to organize during tax season. Digital billing is not just "eco-friendly"; it's a superior business process that saves you time and money.

1. Instant Invoicing

With QTick, you can generate an invoice in 3 clicks and send it directly to the customer's WhatsApp or Email.

No printing, no waiting. It looks professional and modern. Plus, digital invoices are paid 3x faster than paper ones because you can include a direct payment link.

2. Real-Time Inventory Tracking

When you bill a "Shampoo Bottle," your digital system automatically deducts 1 unit from your inventory. You get alerts when stock is low.

You never run out of products, and you never over-order. This level of precision is impossible with manual tracking.

3. Seamless Accounting

At the end of the month, you can export all your sales data to Excel or your accounting software. What used to take days of data entry now takes seconds.

Your accountant will thank you, and you'll have a clear, real-time view of your business's financial health.

4. Customer History

Digital billing builds a history for each client. You can see exactly what they bought last time, how much they spent, and when.

This data is gold for personalized marketing. "Hey Sarah, noticed you bought the Keratin Shampoo 3 months ago. Running low? Here's 10% off your refill."
