Why businesses love Smart Queuing

It's not just about convenience. It's about efficiency and revenue.

Reduce Perceived Wait Time

Customers can wait from anywhere—coffee shop, car, or home. They don't feel "stuck" in your lobby.

Retain Walk-ins

Don't turn people away because of a crowded lobby. Add them to the digital queue and text them when you're ready.

Staff Efficiency

Staff know exactly who is next and what service they need. No more shouting names or confusion.

How it works

1

Customer joins the queue

They scan a QR code at your door or use the QTick app to join remotely.

2

They get real-time updates

They can see their position in line and estimated wait time on their phone.

3

They arrive just in time

We notify them when it's their turn, so they walk in ready to be served.
