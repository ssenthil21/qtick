Why WhatsApp Marketing Beats Email in 2025

Mike Smith
• Nov 29, 2025 • 4 min read

Email open rates have plummeted to around 20%. Meanwhile, WhatsApp boasts a staggering 98% open rate. For local businesses like salons and clinics, the choice is clear.

The Attention Economy

Your customers' inboxes are flooded with newsletters they never read. But their WhatsApp? That's personal. When you send a message there, it gets seen. It's immediate, intimate, and actionable.

Instant Conversions

Email is passive. WhatsApp is conversational. You can send a "Flash Sale: 2 slots left for today!" message and fill those seats in minutes. The friction from "reading" to "booking" is almost zero.

Personalization at Scale

With tools like QTick, you're not just spamming. You're sending personalized birthday wishes, appointment reminders, and follow-ups based on their last visit. "Hey Sarah, it's been 4 weeks since your last color. Want to book a touch-up?"

No Spam Folders

Legitimate business WhatsApp messages land directly in the chat list, not a "Promotions" tab or spam folder. You have a direct line to your customer's pocket.
