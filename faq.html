Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QTick.

General

What is QTick?

QTick is an all-in-one business operating system designed for service-based businesses like salons, spas, clinics, and gyms. It replaces multiple disconnected tools (booking software, billing, marketing) with one unified platform.

Do I need technical skills to use it?

Not at all. QTick is built to be extremely user-friendly. If you can use a smartphone, you can use QTick. We also provide free onboarding support.

Pricing & Billing

Is there a free version?

Yes, our Starter plan is completely free forever. It includes basic appointment booking and queue management for small businesses.

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Absolutely. You can change your plan at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.
